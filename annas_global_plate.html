Our Story

Where Every Dish Tells a Story

At Anna's Global Plate, we believe great food knows no borders. Born from a love of authentic South Indian cuisine and a passion for beloved Canadian comfort classics, we bring you a menu that travels the world without leaving Edmonton.

From hand-crafted, golden-crisp Dosas paired with fragrant Sambar to hearty Butter Chicken Poutine and flaky Fish & Chips, every plate is made with care, honesty, and bold flavour. Whether you grew up eating Idly for breakfast or discovered Perogies on a Canadian winter night, there's a seat at Anna's table for you.
